Top 10 Best T20 Players of all time :-


Rohit Sharma

Rohit Sharma (born 30 April 1987) is an Indian cricketer and the captain of national cricket team in Test and ODI formats. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest opening batters of all time.[3] He is also known for hitting sixes.
